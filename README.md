In the context of the global shift towards sustainable finance, this project conducts a comparative analysis between **Clean Energy (ICLN)** and **Fossil Fuels (XLE)** over a 5-year period. ## ğŸ“Š Key Findings

### 1. Growth vs. Stability
* **Clean Energy (ICLN):** Showed explosive growth during 2020-2021 but exhibited higher volatility (risk).
* **Fossil Fuels (XLE):** Demonstrated resilience and strong recovery during geopolitical crises (e.g., 2022 energy crisis), acting as a hedge against inflation.

### 2. Correlation & Diversification
* The correlation between the two sectors was found to be **low to moderate**.
* **Implication:** Adding Green Energy to a traditional portfolio provides excellent **diversification benefits**, reducing overall portfolio risk.
